Scaling decisions in self-hosted infrastructure involve more than capacity. The question is whether a service is worth the operational overhead of running it yourself — and at what point external alternatives become the pragmatic choice.

This section covers scale decisions at the system, service, and infrastructure level.

## Questions this section addresses

- When does self-hosting make sense, and when does it not?
- How do you manage growth without accumulating operational debt?
- When is a managed or cloud service the right call?
- How do you migrate workloads without data loss or extended downtime?
